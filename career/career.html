Heavy Duty Equipment Technician Job Description – Plant Mechanical Technician

Heavy equipment mechanic to inspect, service, and maintain range of heavy-duty construction equipment, Candidate should have proven experience working with heavy construction machinery, able to troubleshoot, repair the company equipment manufacturer’s standards.

Expected Responsibilities are, but not limited to:

  • Inspect trucks. And other heavy construction equipment for proper performance.
  • Adjust and replace faulty parts as per manufacturer standard.
  • Repair & Overhaul damaged parts / Components.
  • Clean, lubricate, and conduct routine services on trucks.
  • Attend to breakdowns as reported and on immediate basis.
  • Report damaged or faulty equipment to management.
  • Ensure that all repairs are conducted as defined by manufacturer.
  • Ensure work practice conforms to safety regulations and procedures.
  • Ensure order and cleanliness of workshop facilities and all equipment.
  • Ensure there is no any missing equipment or item in the company

Desirable:

  • Minimum 8 years of relevant professional experience in Heavy Construction Equipment .
  • Must be accurate in dealing with the grains to prevent spoilage
  • Strong Personality, Able to carry out duties independently with Minimum supervision.
